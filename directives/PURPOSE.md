# Purpose

A human being in America is linked to https://generalwelfareproject.com/ by some organization

They visit the website via a QR code on a flyer, or a link from another website, or otherwise

They might visit on a low end Android or a high end computer. We should design for both

Here, they can learn about their rights in america

They have a branching point for easy links to important programs

It is a funnel

I do not claim that I can FIX housing, or voting laws, or tenant laws, or trans rights, or free speech

But what I CAN do as a priviledged person in America is to create a website which makes these rights freely and easily accessible

Maybe someone will use it. Maybe no one will ever use it. That's not the point.

The point is that it costs me nothing to get a website like this up and running
