61. Workers' Rights
62. Wage theft, workplace safety, labor board, unemployment
63. Wage theft, workplace safety, labor board complaints, and unemployment insurance.
64. You Are Entitled to Every Dollar You Earned
65. Wage theft is the most common form of theft in America. If you were not paid minimum wage, overtime, or your tips were taken --- the law protects you.
66. You Can Report Unsafe Conditions Confidentially
67. OSHA protects workers. You can file a confidential complaint about unsafe conditions, and your employer cannot legally retaliate.
68. File a Wage Theft Complaint --- No Cost
69. The Department of Labor will investigate your employer at no cost. You do not need a lawyer. File online now.
70. Apply for Unemployment Benefits
71. If you lost your job, you likely qualify. Every state has an online application. You have paid into this system --- these are benefits you have earned.

74. Food
75. Food stamps, food banks, school meals, hunger hotline
76. Food assistance, SNAP benefits, food banks, and free school meals.
77. You May Already Qualify for Food Assistance
78. Millions of Americans qualify for SNAP (food stamps) but never apply. If you are struggling to afford groceries, you may be eligible for monthly benefits --- no matter your age or family size.
79. Your Children Can Eat Free at School
80. If your family has a low income, your children can get free breakfast and lunch every school day. This is a federal program --- every public school in America participates.
81. Find Free Food Near You
82. Food banks give out free groceries --- no ID, no paperwork, no questions asked. Enter your zip code to find one near you right now.
83. Apply for SNAP (Food Stamps)
84. SNAP puts money on a card every month for groceries. You can apply online through your state. If you qualify, benefits can start within 30 days.
